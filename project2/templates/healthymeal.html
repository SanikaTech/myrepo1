Healthy Meal

Here are some food good enough for your pet:-


  • 1. Lean-cooked chicken or turkey.

  • 2. Cooked eggs (without seasoning)

  • 3. Plain, unsweetened yogurt (in moderation)

  • 4. Carrots (cooked or raw)

  • 5. Green beans (cooked and without salt)

  • 6. Apples (seeds and core removed)

  • 7. Pumpkin (plain, cooked, and unsweetened)

  • 8. Blueberries (in moderation)
